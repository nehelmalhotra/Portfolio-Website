Covid 19 - Time series analysis and clustering

Covid-19 has impacted millions of lives and getting the right information at the right time is more critical than ever. In this project, I aim to visualize the spread of Covd-19 and conduct document clustering and topic modelling on a dataset of over 400,000 scholary articles on the coronavirus.

Amazon - Review prediction using NLP

Amazon has been transforming the way we shop and the pandemic has given a huge boost to the online retail business. In this project we use Natural Language Processing and Neural Networks to predict the rating a person might give to a product.

House Price Prediction - Using ensembling and Tableau

The hosuing market has been really hot the past few years due to the really low interest rates. In the project we will train a supervised model to predict the house prices in Boston based on parameters such as number of rooms, area, year built, etc. We will use Tableau for the Exploratory analysis and ensembling techniques to predict the final prices!
